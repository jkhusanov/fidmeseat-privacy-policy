# FindMeSeat — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-05_

FindMeSeat is a mobile app that combines an in-app web browser with a native
seat-pricing dashboard. This policy explains what the app does and does not do
with your information.

## The short version

**FindMeSeat has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no backend of its
own, and it does not sell or share your personal data.** Everything it computes, it
computes on your device. The **one** exception: if the app crashes or hits an error,
it sends an anonymous crash report to **Sentry**, a third-party error-monitoring
service, so we can find and fix bugs (see "Crash reporting" below).

## What stays on your device

- **App settings** (selected site, scan preferences, a randomly generated anonymous
  visitor ID used only for local scan tuning, and whether you've seen the intro) are
  saved locally on your device. They are never uploaded.
- **Pricing/availability data** shown in the dashboard is derived in-app from the
  responses your own logged-in browsing session already receives, and is held only in
  memory and local storage on your device.
- You can clear all of this at any time from **Settings → Clear cache**, or by
  deleting the app.

## The in-app browser

FindMeSeat includes a web browser so you can sign in to and use a third-party
ticketing portal with **your own account**. When you use that browser:

- You interact directly with that third party's website. Your activity there — including
  any sign-in — is governed by **that website's own privacy policy and terms**, not this
  one.
- FindMeSeat does not store your username, password, or payment details. Authentication
  happens entirely between you and the third-party website inside the browser view.

## Crash reporting (the only data that leaves your device)

FindMeSeat uses **Sentry** (Functional Software, Inc.) to capture diagnostics **only
when the app crashes or throws an error** — not during normal use. A crash report may
include:

- the error and its stack trace;
- your device model and OS version, the app version/build, and available memory;
- an anonymous, randomly-generated installation identifier;
- a short trail of in-app "breadcrumbs" (e.g. which screen was open) leading up to the crash.

Sentry is configured **not to store IP addresses**, so no location is collected. This data
is processed by Sentry on servers in the **United States**, is used **solely**
to diagnose and fix crashes, is **never** used for advertising, tracking, or profiling,
and is **never** sold. A crash report never includes your name, your FIFA username,
password, or payment details, or your browsing activity in the in-app browser.

There is no other analytics or telemetry. Apart from crash reports, the app's own code
makes no network calls — all other traffic is the in-app browser loading your own session.

## Data sharing

Other than the anonymous crash reports described above (shared only with Sentry, our
crash-reporting processor), FindMeSeat shares no data with the developer or any other party.

## Children

FindMeSeat is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change. Material changes will
